My Design Approach

Empathy & Ideate

I believe in to gain an empathic understanding of the problem, empathy allows me to set aside my own assumptions and creating many ideas in ideation sessions. I like to "think outside the box" to identify new solutions to the problem statement.

Innovate & Data Driven

Identify opportunities to innovate - User research and prototyping insights help me generate ideas. This is where the design really comes to life. My goal is to make design better than before and for an enjoyable experience. I observe and understand the specific condition in which the solution will be used.
